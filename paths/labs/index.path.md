# Build Week Labs

Labs are safe experiments. They may be incomplete, wrong, or discarded.

---

## Experiment Card

Before starting a lab, record:

- question or hypothesis;
- owner and reviewer;
- starting version or commit;
- files owned and not owned;
- visible result;
- verification;
- stop condition.

---

## Promotion Rule

Promote a lab only when it strengthens the canonical learner journey and
another teammate can reproduce it. Preserve useful failures in
[Reports](../reports/index.path.md).

---

## Candidate Labs

- learner-evidence and tutoring-flow evaluation;
- agent authoring and complete-block live updates;
- interactive data-science learning component;
- clean installation and first-run experience.

Choose owners during the team kickoff rather than assigning them here in
advance.
