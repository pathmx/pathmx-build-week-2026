# Data Science Golden Loop

This lab tests one complete “teach me anything” episode. The learner studies a
small idea, answers a diagnostic question, completes a practice checklist, and
receives a durable next step based on that evidence.

**Learning outcome:** explain why the median often describes a typical value
better than the mean when a dataset contains an extreme outlier.

---

## Start With The Learner

Read the generic [learner profile](./learner.persona.md). In a real session,
Codex confirms the goal and time budget before changing it.

---

## Build The Model

Study [Center Under Pressure](./center-under-pressure.guide.md), including the
interactive outlier model.

---

## Make A Prediction

Answer the [diagnostic question](./choose-center.quiz.md) before doing the
calculation.

---

## Practice

Complete the [delivery-time investigation](./practice.assignment.md). The
task list records the learner's submitted work state in readable Markdown.

---

## Adapt

Ask Codex to review the submitted diagnostic and practice Sources, then update
[Your Next Step](./next-step.guide.md) with either remediation or a transfer
challenge. The evidence Sources remain unchanged.

---

## Resume

Close the Player, reopen this path, and confirm that the diagnostic answer,
practice completion, and adapted next step are still present.
