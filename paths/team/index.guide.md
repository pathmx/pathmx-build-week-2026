# Team Working Agreements

Keep the structure firm enough to prevent collisions and loose enough for each
person to shape their contribution.

The lanes below are a proposed kickoff default, not a permanent org chart.
Accept or change them together before the first work block.

---

## Ownership

- One deliverable has one owner and one reviewer.
- The owner chooses the implementation within the accepted outcome and file
  boundary.
- The reviewer responds with evidence and questions rather than silently
  rewriting the work.

---

## Daily Rhythm

- Morning: freeze one outcome per person.
- Midday: report blockers and dependency changes.
- Evening: demonstrate an artifact and accept, revise, cut, or roll it over.

New ideas enter the project hub or a lab immediately but wait for the next
daily checkpoint unless they resolve a blocker.

## Communication

Use the agreed private chat channel for pings and pairing. Record decisions,
tasks, test results, and accepted changes in this repository so no one must
reconstruct the project from chat history.
